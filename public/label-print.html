First-time setup: In Windows, open Devices and Printers → right-click the Zebra GC420t → Printing Preferences → set paper size to 54 × 26 mm.
Then click Print Label, select the Zebra printer, and click Print.
Use X/Y Offset above to nudge position if needed — it saves automatically.
